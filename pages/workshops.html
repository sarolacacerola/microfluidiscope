About our workshops

We offer workshops for schools that want to test the Microfluidiscope. Each session guides students through the assembly and operation of the device, from running their first microalgae sample to observing cell separation in real time.

Host a workshop

Want to run a Microfluidiscope workshop in your school or community space? We support independent facilitators with materials, documentation, and remote guidance from the team.
